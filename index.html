— What is Virgo

Farm data turned into action.

Virgo is a unified platform that continuously collects field data — soil moisture, crop health, hyperlocal weather — and translates it into real-time profit insights with automated irrigation and fertilizer control.

Built by aerospace engineers passionate about sensors and automation. Designed for India's medium and large-scale farms adopting precision agriculture.
